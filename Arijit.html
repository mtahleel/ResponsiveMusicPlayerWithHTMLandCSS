Arijit Singh

Indian Singer - Music Composer

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian singer and music composer.[6][4][2] He sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali, but has also performed in various other Indian languages.[7][8] He is the recipient of a National Award and six Filmfare Awards. He is often cited as one of the best singers in Indian music industry, and has established a huge fan base throughout South Asia, predominantly in India, Pakistan, Bangladesh and Nepal. He is also known as the "King of Playback Singing".[9][10]
